# CRM Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze CRM data to gain insights into customer behavior, sales performance, lead conversion, and revenue generation. The dashboard helps sales teams and management monitor key business metrics, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

## Dataset Used

The project utilizes CRM datasets containing:

- <a herf="https://github.com/OmPatil078/Data-Analysis-CRM-Project/blob/main/Accont.xlsx">Accounts Data </a>
- <a herf="https://github.com/OmPatil078/Data-Analysis-CRM-Project/blob/main/Lead.xlsx">Lead Data </a>
- <a herf="https://github.com/OmPatil078/Data-Analysis-CRM-Project/blob/main/Opportunity%20Product.xlsx">Opportunity Product Data </a>
- <a herf="https://github.com/OmPatil078/Data-Analysis-CRM-Project/blob/main/Opportunity%20Table.xlsx">Opportunity Table Data </a>
- <a herf="https://github.com/OmPatil078/Data-Analysis-CRM-Project/blob/main/User%20Table.xlsx">User Table Data </a>

## Business Questions (KPIs)

* What is the total number of Leads, Accounts, and Opportunities?
* What is the Lead Conversion Rate?
* Which sales representatives generate the highest revenue?
* What are the top-performing products by revenue and quantity sold?
* Which opportunity stages contribute the most to the sales pipeline?
* What is the Win/Loss Rate of opportunities?
* Which industries and regions generate the highest revenue?
* How does revenue trend over time?

## Dashboard Features

* Executive Summary Dashboard
* Lead Conversion Analysis
* Opportunity Pipeline Tracking
* Revenue Performance Monitoring
* Product Sales Analysis
* Sales Representative Performance
* Interactive Filters and Slicers

## Process

1. Collected and integrated CRM datasets from multiple sources.
2. Performed data cleaning and transformation using SQL and Power Query.
3. Created relationships between Leads, Accounts, Opportunities, Products, and Users.
4. Developed KPIs and DAX measures for business performance tracking.
5. Built interactive Power BI dashboards with dynamic filtering capabilities.
6. Generated actionable insights for sales optimization and customer management.

## Project Insights

* Identified high-performing sales representatives and revenue-generating accounts.
* Analyzed lead conversion patterns to improve sales effectiveness.
* Tracked opportunity stages to monitor pipeline health and forecast revenue.
* Evaluated product performance based on sales volume and revenue contribution.
* Discovered trends in customer acquisition and sales growth across regions.

## Final Conclusion

The CRM Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the sales pipeline, customer relationships, and revenue performance. By leveraging data-driven insights, businesses can improve lead conversion, optimize sales strategies, enhance customer retention, and drive sustainable revenue growth.
